Doraemon is a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Fujiko F.
The main character, Doraemon, is a robotic cat sent from the future to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi.
Doraemon possesses a pocket on his belly that contains various futuristic gadgets and tools to aid Nobita in his everyday life.
The series explores themes of friendship, perseverance, and the consequences of Nobita's actions with the help of Doraemon's inventions.
Doraemon has gained international acclaim for its heartwarming stories and has become an iconic figure in Japanese pop culture.
